Who We Are

Pakistan Virtual Airlines (PVA) is a premier virtual organization operating on the Infinite Flight platform. We are a community of seasoned aviators dedicated to simulating the rich heritage of Pakistan International Airlines.

Our Mission

To provide our pilots with an authentic aviation career path that mirrors real-world procedures, fostering a culture of discipline, skill development, and professional pride in every flight.
