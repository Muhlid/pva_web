Who We Are

Pakistan Virtual Airlines (PVA) is an elite virtual organization operating on Infinite Flight. We are a collective of dedicated aviators committed to simulating the rich legacy of Pakistan International Airlines.

Our Mission

To provide our pilots with an authentic aviation career path that mirrors real-world procedures, fostering a culture of discipline, skill development, and professional pride in every flight.

Our Vision

To remain the most respected virtual carrier in the digital skies, setting the benchmark for community engagement and simulation accuracy across the global Infinite Flight community.
